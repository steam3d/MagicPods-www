MagicPods
Add a little magic to your AirPods

How does it work?
  • Turn on bluetooth.
  • Bring the Airpods to your PC and open them.
  • Watch the magic happen.
Still not working?
  • Check your bluetooth. It must be version 4.0 or above.
  • Turn on startup. If you skip this step or disable startup, go to Task Manager and turn on MagicPods Service.
  • Reboot your system or relaunch app.
  • Go to «How it's work?»
BLE doesn't support
  • Error says that the Magic Pods doesn't have access to Bluetooth. This error cannot be fixed programmatically, it is connected specifically with your PC. However, you can try a few steps.
  • Restart the app one or two times. If you hide tray icon you can stop MagicPodsService.exe from Task Manager.
  • Turn off / on bluetooth and restart the app
  • Try update bluetooth drivers
  • Reboot your PC
